Current and completed research projects within Hitrac include:

Scorecard Portal

This web-based portal serves as a performance feedback mechanism to the community, healthcare facility and community stakeholders. Performance can be viewed in comparison to other districts and can further disaggregate down to health facilities.

Learn More here

ODK (Open Data Kit)

The advancement of information technology has opened up exciting new opportunities for field data gathering and study technique.One of these technologies is Mobile Data Collection (MDC), which collects field data using the Open Data We have collaborated with organizations such as Ycheck, MAITA, and ZimFEPT.

Learn More here

Certificate Verification System(CVS)

The Certificate Verification System is a web-based application developed by the Health Informatics Training & Research Advancement Center (HITRAC). It allows organizations to verify the authenticity of certificates by interfacing with institutional databases where original certificates are housed.

Learn More here

Village Health Workers System(VHWR)

The Health Informatics Training And Research Advancement Center (HITRAC) developed the Village Health Worker Register which is a web-based application, intended for the management of community health workers at national, provincial, district and health facility level.....
